Our kitchen · Ridge, Accra

Our story

Rooted in Ghana,
refined for today.

Coast & Clay opened in 2019 with one idea: Ghanaian food deserves the same care and intention as any great cuisine. Our chefs grew up eating these dishes — they know what they're supposed to taste like. Every recipe starts there.

Alongside our Ghanaian roots, we offer carefully chosen continental dishes held to the same standard — honest, well-made, and worth your time.
